BHS Girls Soccer Clinic

Run by current high school soccer players

Want to keep your kids active?


Sign your kids up for the BHS Girls Soccer Clinic, where your kids can play soccer throughout the summer and enjoy it at the same time! Run by current high school players, they will learn the skills to take them to the next level in a fun and enriching way! Sign up today!

Keeping Kids in Soccer


Our goal with this clinic is to keep your kids in soccer throughout the summer. It helps them get ready for the fall season and keeps them active! Additionally, it's a non-profit clinic, and all left over funds will be donated to the BHS Girls Soccer Boosters and/or soccer related charities.

Schedule & Information


  1. For girls, rising 1st-8th graders
  2. $35 for the whole summer! We request that you pay when you submit the form or at the first clinic day. If you have trouble please contact us and we can work it out.
  3. Clinics will be every Friday from 5-6 pm. Perfect for after summer camp! Also, you are not required to attend every session, but make sure to let us know 24 hours prior to the session!
  4. Here is a link to our google calendar for the clinic schedule and the high school team's fall schedule!

During the clinic we will...


  1. Do dribbling drills
  2. Work on passing and teamwork skills
  3. Shoot! (a lot)
  4. And most importantly... have fun!
